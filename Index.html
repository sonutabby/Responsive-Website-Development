WE ARE VALORANT

VALORANT is a global arena in which you can compete.
It is a 5v5 tactical shooter in which players can take turns planting and defusing spikes
with a one-life-per-round system during a series of 13 rounds. What you'll need more than guns
and bullets is to choose an operator equipped with fast, lethal, and situationally adaptable abilities
to create the right opportunities for you to impress everyone with your superior weapon skills.

E-SPORTS

OVERVIEW

  • The Valorant Community Tournament, powered by Jack Link’s, is an open tournament (the “Tournament”).
  • There will be up to 128 teams in the Tournament. Teams will consist of five players (individually a “Participant” and together a “Team”), one of whom will be designated captain and will be the main point of contact with the Tournament organisers.
  • Participants may stream the Tournament with the permission of the Tournament administrators. Such permission should be sought by contacting Fnatic Ltd (“Fnatic”) on the Tournament Discord or emailing community@fnatic.com.
  • The Tournament is a three (3) day, Valorant tournament. The tournament will consist of a 128 team bracket and will be played on PC.
